This is a GitHub page design to host my SUSI.AI bot made for the SUSI.AI: Add your personal SUSI.AI bot to your Github Pages task by FOSSASIA during Google Code-In 2018. Feel welcome to chat with the bot. I hope you like it even though it has very few reactions. If you get lost here's a list of available queries.

  • Hello|Hi|Howdy|Hey
  • Who are you|Can you introduce yourself
  • Who made you|Who is your creator|Tell me something about your creator
  • Please repeat *|echo * to me
  • I like you|I quite like you| You're nice| You seem nice
  • I do not like you|I quite dislike you| You're awful
  • Because you're *|Because you are * (after the bot learns that the user dislikes it)
  • Why do I not like you
  • Do you work
  • *, * or *
  • Bye|Farewell|Goodbye|See you
